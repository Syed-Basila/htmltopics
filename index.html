Who we are

Saudi AAC Product Company established in October 2010, it has been a pioneer in producing high-quality Autoclaved Aerated Concrete (AAC) blocks. In May 2017, the company modernized its facility with state-of-the-art European-standard machinery, enabling it to deliver premium AAC products to meet the diverse needs of its clients.

  • Fully modernized plant equipped with advanced European machinery ensures superior product quality.
  • Total production capability of 300,000 m³ per year to meet bulk and timely delivery requirements.
  • Dedicated to maintaining excellence in quality and fulfilling client commitments with utmost reliability.

With a total annual production capacity of 300,000 m³, the company is committed to providing reliable, high-standard solutions while adhering to strict quality and quantity benchmarks.
